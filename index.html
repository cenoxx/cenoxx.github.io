The End At Last is a metalcore band originating from Dayton, Ohio.

Built around heavy rhythms, aggressive energy, and emotionally driven songwriting, we blend crushing riffs with a modern, hard-hitting sound.

The End At Last is Parker Buhrman (Vocals/Guitar), Alex Watson (Guitar), Nate VanBuskirk (Bass), and Christian Enochs (Drums).
